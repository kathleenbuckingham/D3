Correlations Discovered Between Smoking and Age

The vast majority of smokers are over the age of 35. Approximately 13% to 25% of people aged between 36 and 44 smoke across all states.

The highest percentage of smokers can be found in West Virginia, Kentucky, and Arkansas. With ages between 38 and 38 years old. The lowest percentage of smokers can be found in Utah, California and Hawaii, with the youngest age range between 31 and 38. The eldest smokers can be found in Maine at an average age of 44 years old.
